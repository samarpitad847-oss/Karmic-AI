# Karmic-AI
Karmic AI

Giving back to those who give everything.

About the Project

Karmic AI is a mental health and burnout prevention platform designed specifically for doctors and medical students in India.

It focuses on providing quick, anonymous, and low-effort support for users who are often too busy or overwhelmed to seek traditional help.

The goal is simple:
Make mental health support accessible in seconds, not sessions.

The Problem
Doctors work long, exhausting shifts with little rest
High levels of burnout, stress, and emotional fatigue
Mental health support is often:
Time-consuming
Stigmatized
Not designed for medical professionals
Women doctors face additional challenges like:
Workplace pressure
Harassment
Work-life imbalance
Our Solution

Karmic AI provides a fast, anonymous, and easy-to-use platform where doctors can:

Check in on their mental health in under 20 seconds
Track mood and burnout patterns over time
Record voice notes instead of typing
Get simple, instant suggestions for relief
Access peer support in a safe environment
Key Features
1. Quick Check-In
One-tap mood, energy, and sleep tracking
No long forms or typing required
2. Voice Journal
Record thoughts using voice
Get simulated emotional feedback
3. Mood Tracking (MoodMirror)
Visualize patterns over time
Identify burnout triggers
4. Peer Support
Anonymous doctor-to-doctor chat
Safe and judgment-free
5. Micro-Interventions
Small, actionable suggestions like:
“Drink water”
“Take a short break”
Design Philosophy
Anonymous by default
Ultra-fast interactions
Minimal effort required
Emotionally safe environment
Built for real-world doctor workflows
Tech Stack (Example)
Frontend: Next.js / React
Styling: Tailwind CSS
Charts: Recharts / Chart.js
Voice: MediaRecorder API
Storage: Local Storage / Firebase
Target Users
Medical students
Interns and residents
Practicing doctors
Especially women in medicine
Future Scope
AI-based burnout prediction
Integration with hospitals
Verified mental health professional network
Regional language support
Expansion to other countries
Vision

To become a quiet, trusted companion for healthcare workers —
something they can rely on, even on their worst days.
